A refined inner product

  • The set of functions we'll be working on is \(H_k\):
    • \(H_k \subset L^2(\mathbb{R}^n)\)
    • Derivatives of all orders less than \(k\) are \(L^2\).

Plancherel Theorem

if \(f\in L^1\cap L^2\) then:

  • the Fourier transform \(\hat f\in L^2\)
  • \(\Vert f\Vert_2 = \Vert \hat f\Vert_2\)

Riemann-Lebesgue Lemma

The Fourier transform of an \(L^1\) function is continuous and vanishes at infinity.
